These are the latest version of UND's APEX Calculus, compiled on 2016-04-05.

On a computer, the interactive version is by far the best, since Adobe's pdf reader allows you to manipulate selected three dimensional graphics. If your bandwidth is limited (or you'll be printing the document anyway), then you may as well download the smaller static, color version. If you'll be printing without color, then download the black and white version, since its graphics have been specifically adjusted.

On a computer, the full Calculus is the best, since everything is correctly hyperlinked. If you'll be printing the document, then you may want to carry around less weight, and should focus on the volume of interest.

The instructor's version is the black and white version with all the problem solutions and an occasional note.
