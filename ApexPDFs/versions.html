These are the latest version of UND's APEX Calculus Late Transcendentals, compiled on 2017-04-11.

On a computer, the interactive version is by far the best, since everything is correctly hyperlinked and Adobe's pdf reader allows you to manipulate selected three dimensional graphics.

If your bandwidth is limited (or you'll be printing the document anyway), then you may as well download the smaller, static, color version of the volume of interest. If you'll be printing without color, then download the black and white version, since its graphics have been specifically adjusted.
