$\vec\ell_2(t) =\bracket{3,3,3}+ t\bracket{-4,2,-2}$.}{parallel}

$\vec\ell_2(t) =\bracket{14,5,9}+ t\bracket{1,1,1}$.}{intersecting; $\ell_1(2) = \ell_2(-2) =\bracket{12,3,7}$}
