    Let {an} be given such that limn→∞⁡|an|=0. By the definition of the limit of a sequence, given any ϵ>0, there is a m such that for all n>m,||an|-0|<ϵ. Since ||an|-0|=|an-0|, this directly implies that for all n>m, |an-0|<ϵ, meaning that limn→∞⁡an=0.
